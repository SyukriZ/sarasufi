Secure Packaging

Your honey is precious to us. We ensure safe delivery through:

  • Bubble wrap protection for glass containers
  • Sturdy corrugated boxes with cushioning
  • Temperature-resistant packaging for heat sensitivity
  • Fragile handling labels for courier awareness

Kelulut honey is best stored in a cool, dry place. Avoid direct sunlight exposure upon delivery.

Order Tracking & Issues

Stay updated on your delivery status:

  • Real-time updates through our courier partners
  • Delivery notifications upon arrival

If you experience any of the following, please contact us immediately:

  • Damaged packaging or leaking containers
  • Incorrect or missing items in your order
