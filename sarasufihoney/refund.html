Refund Policy

We want you to feel confident when you choose SaraSufi Kelulut Premium Gold.

SaraSufiHoney Promise

At SaraSufiHoney, your satisfaction is the priority. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, a 30‑day return and exchange policy is offered for eligible products.

Return Eligibility

Returns are eligible if:

  • The item is in its original condition.
  • Less than 15% of the product has been consumed.

You may visit an authorised outlet or agreed meet‑up point for an exchange. Exchanges can be made for the same item or another item of similar value.

How to Start a Return

Contact the team via email or WhatsApp with your order details. Please include:

1

Order Details

Name, phone & order date

2

Explanation

Brief issue description

3

Photos

Clear images of product

Returns without prior approval will not be accepted. Return instructions will be shared once approved.

Refunds

Refunds are only applicable for products returned in original condition and within the stated period. After the return is received and inspected, you will be notified of the approval status.

If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method. Please allow a few business days for your bank or card provider to complete the transaction.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

Please check your order immediately upon delivery. If any item arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact SaraSufiHoney as soon as possible so the issue can be resolved quickly.

Non‑Returnable Items

All returns are subject to evaluation by our team. Please note:

  • Gift cards or vouchers are non‑refundable.
  • Products that have been consumed more than 15% or heavily tampered with will not be accepted.
