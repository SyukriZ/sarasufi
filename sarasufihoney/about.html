Our Story

About Us

From a small family kitchen to a lifestyle honey brand, SaraSufiHoney was created to make premium kelulut honey part of everyday wellness.

The Journey

From Hive To Home

SaraSufiHoney started with a simple idea – bring honest, lab‑tested kelulut honey from trusted beekeepers to busy Malaysian families in a way that feels modern, beautiful, and easy to enjoy every day.

Natural Origins
Made with Care
Family Focused
Our Mission

Who We Are

We work closely with small local beekeepers who practise responsible stingless‑bee farming. Each batch of honey is collected in small quantities, carefully handled, and sent for independent lab testing before it reaches your home.

Our goal is to make kelulut honey feel approachable – not only as a supplement on the shelf, but as a daily ritual in drinks, cooking, and gifting moments that you share with the people you love.
