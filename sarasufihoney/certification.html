Testing Standards

What We Check For

Moisture & Stability

Moisture is measured to help prevent fermentation and to align with quality expectations for kelulut honey.

Target 18-25%

Sugar Profile

The sugar profile is reviewed to detect adulteration and confirm authentic kelulut honey, not mixed with syrups.

Method HPLC Analysis

Microbiological Safety

Basic microbiological parameters are checked to ensure safety for consumption when stored as recommended.

Standard MS 1484

Pollen & Origin

Pollen analysis confirms the floral source and geographical origin, ensuring authentic kelulut honey traceability.

Method Microscopy
