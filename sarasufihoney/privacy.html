Introduction

Thank you for visiting SaraSufiHoney. Your privacy and the security of your personal information are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you interact with SaraSufiHoney online.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

When you contact us, place an order, or interact with our pages, we may collect information such as:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Delivery address and order details
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment providers)
  • Communication preferences and history

Technical information such as browser type, device information, and general location (for example, city or country) may also be collected through analytics tools to help improve the website experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders and respond to enquiries
  • Provide order updates, shipping notifications, or support
  • Improve products, services, and website content based on feedback and usage
  • Share important updates, promotions, or announcements if you choose to receive them
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities

Sharing of Information

SaraSufiHoney does not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only with trusted service providers, such as delivery partners or payment processors, when needed to complete your order.

In certain situations, information may also be shared to comply with legal obligations or valid requests from authorities.

We implement strict security measures to protect your data when shared with third-party service providers.

Data Retention

Order and contact records may be kept for as long as necessary to support customer service, accounting, or legal requirements. Typically, we retain your personal information for:

  • Active orders: Duration of order processing plus 2 years
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt out or request deletion
  • Legal records: As required by applicable Malaysian law

If you would like your information to be updated or removed, you may contact the team using the details below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask for corrections if any details are inaccurate or incomplete
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

Where applicable, you may also request that certain information be deleted, subject to any legal or operational obligations that require the data to be retained for a period of time.

Cookies & Analytics

The website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the site. You can usually manage cookies through your browser settings.

We use analytics tools to help us understand website traffic and usage patterns. This data is anonymized and used solely for improving our services.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in practices, services, or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes. Significant changes will be notified via email or a prominent notice on our website.
