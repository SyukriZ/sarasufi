Safety First

Safety & Simple Guidelines

Who should avoid honey?

Honey is not recommended for infants under 12 months due to the risk of botulism. Always follow your doctor's advice if you have specific health conditions or allergies.

How much per day?

Most adults enjoy 1–2 tablespoons a day as part of a balanced diet. For children over 1 year, use smaller amounts (1 teaspoon). Remember: moderation is key.

Diabetic customers

Kelulut honey is still a form of sugar. Enjoy in moderation and only according to your healthcare professional's guidance. Monitor your blood sugar levels accordingly.
